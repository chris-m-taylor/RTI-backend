Census Data Analysis Data provided by the US census

In this exercise I was tasked to take an existing database that had been normalized and "flatten" it to a single csv. I then took the data into a python script in order to see what I could infer about it and these are my results.

Advantages of School

For this chart we can see a big increase in the amount of college students that make over 50k vs only having a highschool degree with only 15% making over 50k.

Gender Pay Scale

I wanted to see if within the data provided I was able to see a dramatic difference between the pay of a women and the pay of a man. The data proves that men on average earn 20% more than women.

Employment Wages

While looking at the data I decided to try and see which area of work was the most successful in terms of making over 50,000 dollars a year. I found that the government section outranked both private and self employed but self employed followed closely behind.
